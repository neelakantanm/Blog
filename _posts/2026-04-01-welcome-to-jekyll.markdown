Shourik (also spelled Shurik) is a less known diminutive or pet name for Alexander. Most people are used to the most common diminutive Sasha as I haven't heard many people use it (at least in the math community). It is a friendly, affectionate variation often used by family and friends. I learned about this variation from a post of Valentin Poénaru [Memories of Shourik](https://www.ams.org/notices/200808/tx080800964p.pdf). This was around the time when I was searching for a name for my blog post.
